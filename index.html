I am a PhD student in Computer Science at University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. I am currently advised by Prof. Snigdha Chaturvedi. My research interests revolve around learning semantically rich latent representations for natural language.

Prior to joining my PhD, I worked as Data Scientist II in the Bing AI team at Microsoft. Before that, I spent a year at Apple, working on security engineering. I graduated from IIT Kharagpur with an integrated masters (B.Tech + M.Tech) in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science in 2018.
