I am a Software Engineer at Apple Inc., working on Security Engineering. I graduated from Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur with a Dual Degree (Bachelors+Masters) Hons. in Electrical Engineering, minor in Computer Science and Engineering and micro-specialization in Embedded Wireless Systems (completed 257+ credits/hours of coursework). In my final year, I collaborated with Statistical Machine Learning Group at Indian Institute of Science, tackling problems relating to Knowledge Graphs in NLP, for over a year .

My research interests include Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing, particularly Statistical Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Scalable Deep Learning and its applications in the domain of NLP.
