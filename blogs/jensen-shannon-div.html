Jensen-Shannon (JS) Divergence

Jensen-Shannon divergence is a measure of how similar two probability distributions \(p\) and \(q\) are. This divergence measure is a modification of the KL divergence measure (refer to my previous blog for details). Jensen-Shannon overcomes few of the major limitation of the KL divergence. JS divergence is symmetric and always has a finite value.
